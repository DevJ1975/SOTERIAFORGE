## Notes on subservice (carve-out) controls

Several controls (ENC-1, ENC-2, and aspects of availability) are **provided by Google Cloud /
Firebase** as a subservice organization. In a real SOC 2 report these would be addressed via the
carve-out or inclusive method, with reliance on GCP's own SOC 2 report. They are listed here as
"Implemented" because the platform correctly relies on managed encryption/TLS, but the
**organization** must still obtain and review GCP's (and Stripe's) attestations (control VM-1).
